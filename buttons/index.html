Ant Invasion

To play the game, follow these instructions

The aim of the game is to free the ant colony and let them eat the candy and destroy the leaf!

To free the ant colony, activate the ants!

To add more ants to your colony, press the mouse!

The more ants you add, the easier it will be to destroy the leaf!

Wait for the secret message after the ants destroy the leaf!

The message indicates that you have won the game!
